Pop songs with the same progressions!


Within pop music, there are very popular progressions! The progression that rises above them all however consists of 4 key chords: Am, F, G and C. These chords are all in songs we know well, like "I don’t care" by Ed Sheeran to “I like me better“ by Lauv, and these chords dominate the mainstream. Within this WebApp, you have the option to search the database for a pop song that has this chord progression and you also have the option to add songs you know with this chord progression or even a pop song you like! Happy vibing!
